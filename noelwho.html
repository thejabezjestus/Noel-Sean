ABOUT ME

ACTOR. RAPPER. DANCER AND A FOLLOWER OF JESUS CHRIST

I was born on 28 November in Hyderabad to Samuel and Sarah. I have three siblings. I went on to pursue the Bachelor of Fine arts in SVCFA and became a web designer successfully. I worked for Cognizant as a trainee until I joined the film industry.

Career
I made my first appearance on screen in Sambhavami Yuge Yuge, during which I met Krishna, who later introduced me to the music director M. M. Keeravani. The latter gave me my first break as a Rapper in the Telugu movie Vikramarkudu directed by S. S. Rajamouli.
